History — Spices of the Munnar Hills

Nestled in the Western Ghats, the hills of Munnar have been a treasure for spice cultivation for centuries. Cool climate, rich soil and misty air combine to produce aromatic cardamom, pepper, cinnamon and other premium spices. Traders from Arab lands and Europe visited Kerala for these prized products, making Munnar part of the ancient spice routes. Today, Munnar’s plantations continue this legacy with organic, export-quality produce — preserving traditional aroma and taste.
